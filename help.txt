This program can sort strings of famous "Eugene Onegin" by A. Pushkin.
1. It writes in output.txt original poem.
2. Then, it sorts all strings in lexicographic order from the beginning skipping all punctiation marks and spaces.
For example: "AB><>! and AC is sorted in lexicographic order
3. Finally, it sorts all strings in lexicographic order frome the end skipping all punctuation marks and spaces/
For example: "<>AB<"""AAA and "<>!@<>$$>@<!<!AAB is sorted in lexicographic order.
P.S. Each part of output.txt is diveded by "----------END OF PART------------"
